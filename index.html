Reading List: Probability and Statistics

Probabilistic Reasoning

  • Probability Theory: The Logic of Science by E. T. Jaynes
  • Introduction to Probability Models (10th Edition) by Sheldon M. Ross
  • Probabilistic Reasoning in Intelligent Systems: Networks of Plausible Inference by Judea Pearl
  • Probabilistic Graphical Models: Principles and Techniques by Daphne Koller and Nir Friedman

Statistics

  • Introduction to the Practice of Statistics (7th Edition) by David Moore, George P. McCabe & Bruce Craig
  • Statistics (4th Edition) David Freedman & Robert Pisani & Roger Purves
  • All of Statistics: A Concise Course in Statistical Inference by Larry Wasserman
  • Statistical Learning Theory by Vladimir N. Vapnik
  • Probability and Statistics (4th Edition) by Morris H. Degroot and Mark J. Schervish
  • Introduction to Mathematical Statistics (7th Edition) by Robert V. Hogg, Joeseph McKean & Allen T Craig
  • The Elements of Statistical Learning: Data Mining, Inference, and Prediction (2nd Edition) by Trevor Hastie, Robert Tibshirani & Jerome Friedman
  • NIST/SEMATECH e-Handbook of Statistical Methods by NIST
  • Statistical Pattern Recognition (3rd Edition) by Andrew R. Webb, Keith D. Copsey

Bayesian Statistics

  • Standford: Stats 270/370: A Course in Bayesian Statistics by Chiara Sabatti
  • Bayesian Statistics: An Introduction (3rd edition) by Peter M. Lee
  • Bayesian Data Analysis (2nd Edition) by Andrew Gelman, John B. Carlin, Hal S. Stern & Donald B. Rubin
  • Understanding Computational Bayesian Statistics by William M. Bolstad
  • A First Course in Bayesian Statistical Methods by Peter D. Hoff

Markov Chain Monte Carlo

  • An Introduction to MCMC for Machine Learning by Christophe Andrieu, Nando de Freitas, Arnaud Doucet & Michael I. Jordan
